Iyoghẹ Agro Ventures (Waste Management & Agro-innovation)

Iyoghẹ Agro Ventures transforms livestock and agricultural waste — especially from poultry and animal farms — into valuable, eco-friendly resources. We partner with farmers, agribusinesses, and communities to collect waste, process it sustainably, and produce organic fertilizers, biofuels, and soil enhancers. We address environmental pollution, improve soil health, and support food security in Nigeria and beyond.

Key Services:
  • Farm waste collection & transport
  • Waste-to-product processing
  • Distribution of organic and eco-friendly agricultural inputs
  • Farmer training and community outreach
